F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Need quick assistance? View our FAQs for a solution! If you need further assistance, please contact us through our Contact Form

How long does shipping usually take?

We try our best to send orders out within 24 hours of receiving them. Depending on your location, it may take between 7-14 business days, excluding any unexpected delays.

What kinds of metals are used in the jewelry?

Moonstone Jewels is jewelry that will last. Our materials range from Sterling Silver, Stainless Steel, Titanium, and 18K-24K gold vermeil.

Is Moonstone Jewels hypoallergenic?

Sterling Silver and stainless steel have traces of nickel. If you have nickel or alloy sensitivities, we'd recommend our gold-plated collection or titanium pieces!

Do you offer repairs for any damaged pieces?

We're confident that our jewelry pieces will last! However, if your beloved Moonstone Jewel has any scratches or blemishes, we currently offer repairs at select locations. If a location is not near you, please contact one of our stores directly for further instructions on how we can assist you!

Do you take commissions or requests?

While we love getting new ideas, we are currently unable to take any special orders or requests. Thank you for your consideration!

What do I do if I haven't received my order yet?

Please use our contact form for any issues with your orders.
